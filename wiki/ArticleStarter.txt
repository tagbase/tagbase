=== Step 1: Download the Starter Kit from our [http://code.google.com/p/tagbase/downloads/list Downloads] page ===

Or use the link here -

=== Step 2: Unzip the zip file into your disk drive, e.g. C:\ ===

You should then have the following file structure in `C:\Tagbase`
  * Images
  * plugins
  * Shapes
  * tagfiles
  * templates
